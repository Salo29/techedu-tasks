VISION AND MISSION

High school students learn in their own unique ways, so we help each one customize their plans to achieve a solid standards-based education in a friendly way taking advantage of all the appropriate modern technology tools. We help you take responsibility for your own current and future learning through developing your academic, interpersonal and technological skills.

Company vision statement: Preparing each student to succeed in a rapidly changing world.

Company Mission: As an independent study/home school program, Visions In Education utilizes a standards-based education that addresses individual differences and learning styles. We empower students to take ownership and responsibility for their present and future learning by developing their academic, interpersonal, intrapersonal, and technological skills.
